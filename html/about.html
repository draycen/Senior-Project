About Us

Located in Ho Chi Minh City, a 14-minute walk from Fine Arts Museum and 0.6 miles from the center, Home Away Homestay, City Center, D1 has air-conditioned accommodations with free Wifi, and free bikes. All self-catered units feature parquet floors and are fitted with a TV, an equipped kitchen with a fridge, and a private bathroom with bidet. Some units feature a seating area and/or a balcony. The homestay has a terrace. Popular points of interest near Home Away Homestay, City Center, D1 include Takashimaya Vietnam, Ben Thanh Street Food Market and Tao Dan Park. The nearest airport is Tan Son Nhat International, 5 miles from the accommodation, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
